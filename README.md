A consulting-style digital transformation strategy for a mid-sized retail bank facing slowing digital adoption, rising fintech competition, and increasing operational costs. This project applies structured consulting frameworks to diagnose business challenges, evaluate strategic options, and develop an implementation roadmap with quantified financial impact.

---

## Executive Summary

Meridian Bank has experienced a decline in digital adoption growth while fintech competitors continue to capture younger and digitally native customers.

This case study develops an **18-month transformation strategy** focused on:

- AI-powered underwriting
- Branch network optimization
- Embedded fintech partnerships

The proposed strategy aims to improve customer experience, accelerate loan decisioning, optimize operational costs, and drive sustainable long-term growth.

---

## Business Problem

### Current Challenges

- Digital adoption growth slowed from **15% to 4%** over four years.
- Loan approval requires **5–7 days**, compared to **less than 24 hours** at fintech lenders.
- Cost-to-income ratio remains above industry benchmarks.
- Traditional branches continue to create high fixed operating costs.
- Neobanks are rapidly acquiring younger customers.

---

## Project Objectives

- Increase digital adoption
- Improve customer acquisition and retention
- Reduce operational costs
- Accelerate lending decisions
- Build scalable digital banking capabilities
- Deliver measurable financial returns

## Root Cause Analysis

Identified six primary drivers behind declining growth:

- Legacy banking systems
- Branch-heavy operating model
- Fragmented customer data
- Slow underwriting process
- Fintech competition
- Regulatory complexity

# Strategic Recommendations

## 1. AI-Powered Underwriting

**Objective**

Reduce loan decision time from several days to a few hours.

**Expected Impact**

- +6% new accounts
- Faster credit decisions
- Better customer experience

---

## 2. Branch Network Optimization

**Objective**

Transform physical branches into digital advisory hubs.

**Expected Impact**

- Lower operating costs
- Improved service quality
- Better resource allocation

---

## 3. Embedded Finance Partnerships

**Objective**

Expand customer acquisition through API-based fintech integrations.

**Expected Impact**

- +8% new-to-bank customers
- Greater market reach
- Increased ecosystem engagement
